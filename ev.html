Overview

Evidence vault is a high secure file-sharing management application building in March Networks Command Enterprise System New Generation platform. It allows users to share highly confidential cases or files outside of their networks safely without other portable media options.

In 2020, I started to work on EV from sketches to high fidelity prototype, and the team delivered the first release of the product by the end of the year. Recently, we are working on the UI improvement and adding a new feature which is Media Export to Evidence Vault. Unfortunately due to NDAs, I am unable to display the latest work on my site. However, I am happy to disucss the design process, workflow and challenges about the project.
